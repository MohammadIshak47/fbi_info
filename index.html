All industries

Healthcare and Life Sciences

Since 2009, Innowise has been delivering a wide range of custom and platform-based medical solutions, including patient & hospital apps, telemedicine apps, EHR/EMR systems, software for medical devices, IoMT solutions, etc. We ensure robust security of the designed software and regulation compliance (i.e, HIPAA). Digital care is one of the business priority areas of our company.

Finance, Banking and Insurance

Our company delivers top-notch BFSI software covering all the phases of the development process, from product ideation and project planning to solution roll-out and support. Innowise’s team has deep expertise in building digital banking solutions, mobile banking apps, lending platforms, blockchain-based apps, investment & trading solutions, etc.

eCommerce, Retail and Consumer Goods

Innowise provides both online and offline retail businesses with advanced eCommerce solutions (custom and platform-based). Our retail software products facilitate sales operations, contribute to organizing personalized offers, and help achieve better customer experience.

eLearning

Innowise is engaged in the implementation of modern technologies in the education field and creates a full set of educational software from scratch. Custom eLearning solutions we develop meet all modern trends, stand out for their user-friendliness, correspond to an interactive educational approach, and have AI capabilities.

Logistics and Transportation

Innowise develops custom Logistics & Transportation management solutions aimed at optimizing employee performance, vehicle maintenance, freight management, and hence raising business revenue. We integrate the designed software with the customers’ systems and ensure compliance with governmental regulations.

Hi-Tech and Innovations

We offer our extensive technology expertise to R&D departments, Independent Software Vendors and Hi-Tech corporations to drive the digital future together. Innowise offers a broad spectrum of IT services, from code audit and software engineering to quality assurance and support. Also, we participate in technically complex, non-linear and knowledge-intensive projects for the development of groundbreaking solutions.

Automotive

Innowise offers automotive software development services to all industry players. The automotive industry is developing at a fast pace, so our company understands the need and importance of developing advanced solutions that can facilitate vehicle data management and exchange.

Media and Entertainment

We leverage our technology expertise to develop highly interactive applications for the Media and Entertainment industry. Our Media & Entertainment solutions can help you expand your share in a highly competitive market, maximize ROI, and make significant contributions. We develop software for all possible sectors of the M&E field, including music, video, interpersonal communication, digital media.
